Our Story

A Cafe That Ignites Your Senses

Nestled on the 2nd floor of Sambhav Palace, Fire & Flare Cafe & Lounge is Jaunpur's most vibrant dining destination — known for good WiFi, elaborate menu, customizable food, fresh food, and great quality.

With over 3,600 reviews and a 4.1★ rating on Zomato, we've become the go-to spot at Jaycess Chauraha for food lovers across Jaunpur.
